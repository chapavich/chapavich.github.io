About

Phil_wc or Chapavich Temnitikul is a Thai film score & multimedia music composer and experimental artist.

Mainly, Phil composes music for films, games, TV advertise and stage show. Moreover, He is a multi-genres composer who makes music in many genres such as Electronic, EDM, Trance, Electro House, Experimental, Noise, and Ambient.

Biography

     Chapavich Temnitikul or Phil was born in 1991, in Bangkok, Thailand, he began studying piano at the age of 6. Phil was interested in music composition. Eventually, he took class and graduated in music composition from Rangsit University. Phil started composing for film when he was 19. While he was studying, he has done great deals of films and media in Thailand. Some of his works also were shown in many worldwide film festivals. In 2010, he got an award in film scoring competition that persuaded him to be a film composer. Moreover, he got a scolarship to do a film scoring workshop at FilmSoundHamburg, Germany in 2012 with Jan A. P. Kaczmarek.

     Not only film music, but Phil is also working on experimental music on his own. He does live performance and produce ambient and experimental music. He has composed and listened to music in various styles such as Electronic Dance Music, experimental, progressive, and avant-garde music. Because of wide-range of interested, he can work for both commercial and art. Currently, Phil is being a full time student at University of California, Los Angeles Extension in film scoring in 2014.
